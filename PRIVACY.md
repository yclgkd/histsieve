# Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-05-19

HistSieve is designed to clean browser history locally in Chrome.

## Data Handled

HistSieve handles:

- Browser history URLs and page titles, only for matching and deletion.
- User-created keyword rules.
- Cleanup settings, such as interval and age threshold.
- Last cleanup timestamp.

## Data Collection

HistSieve does not collect, transmit, sell, rent, or share user data.

No browsing history, keyword rules, settings, or cleanup results are sent to any
server controlled by the developer or by a third party.

## Storage

Settings and keyword rules are stored locally with Chrome extension storage on the
user's device. They are used only to provide the extension's history cleanup
features.

## Permissions

HistSieve uses the `history` permission to find and delete browser history entries
that match user-configured rules. It uses `alarms` for scheduled cleanup and
`storage` for local settings.

## Limited Use

HistSieve uses browser history data only to provide and improve its single
purpose: cleaning browser history according to the user's local settings.

HistSieve does not use user data for advertising, analytics, profiling, or
personalized recommendations.
